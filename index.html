My academic work focuses on the interaction between geometry, analysis, and physics. I am particularly interested in variational principles, geometric structures, and how abstract objects can be represented in concrete and visual ways.

  • Hyperbolic geometry and group actions
  • Visualization of algebraic structures using MATLAB
